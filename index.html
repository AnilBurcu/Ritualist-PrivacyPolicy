Privacy Policy for Ritualist

Last Updated: June 2025

Developer: Perikles

1. Information Collected

The Ritualist app itself does not collect personal data directly. However, it uses third-party services that may collect certain information:

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication purposes, basic user data such as name and email address may be collected.
  • Apple Sign-In: May collect basic user authentication details.
  • Push Notifications: The app requests permission to send notifications using Expo Notifications.
  • Supabase: Used to manage backend data and user sessions.
  • Local Storage: Information such as preferences and session tokens may be stored locally on the device.

2. Permissions Used

The app may request the following permissions:

  • android.permission.INTERNET – Required to connect to online services.
  • android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS – To send push notifications.
  • android.permission.VIBRATE – Used for notification vibration.

Note: Although RECORD_AUDIO is listed in the manifest, the app does not use microphone functionality.

3. How We Use Data

Any data retrieved from third-party services is used only to enable core app functionality, such as login and notifications. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

4. Security

We implement secure protocols, including SSL encryption, to safeguard user data.

This policy may be updated. Any changes will be posted on this page.
